AI Bot – Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect
The bot may collect and store the following types of data:
- Messages sent to the bot (for functionality, moderation, and debugging).
- User IDs, timestamps, and command usage.
- Error logs and system activity related to bot performance.
The bot does not intentionally collect sensitive personal information. Users should avoid sharing such data.

2. How Your Data Is Used
Data collected by the bot may be used for:
- Responding to user commands.
- Improving bot performance and accuracy.
- Moderation, abuse prevention, and security.
- Debugging or fixing issues.
No data is sold, rented, or shared with third-party companies.

3. Data Sharing
Data may be shared only with:
- Server moderators/administrators when necessary for safety or enforcement.
- Bot developers for troubleshooting or improvement.
The data will never be shared publicly or used for advertising.

4. Data Retention
- Logs may be stored temporarily for debugging and moderation.
- Data may be deleted during updates, resets, or maintenance.
- Long-term retention is minimized whenever possible.

5. User Responsibility
Users should avoid sending personal, private, or sensitive information to the bot.
Any information voluntarily shared is done at the user's own risk.

6. Security
Reasonable measures are used to protect collected data, but no system is completely secure.
The bot developers are not liable for data breaches caused by external factors.

7. Children’s Privacy
The bot is not designed for users under 13. Supervision is recommended for younger users in accordance with Discord’s guidelines.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy
This policy may be updated at any time. Continued use of the bot means you accept the latest version.
